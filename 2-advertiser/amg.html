Roller Doors

Gliderol domestic roller doors are available in a variety of sizes up to 5m wide and 3m high.  Because the curtain sits behind the brickwork they are ideal for arched or unusual garage openings. The doors do not require a frame meaning maximum possible garage door opening width.

Door goes up, door goes down.

Manual Doors

Most doors are available in both manual and electrically driven automatic. Whether you choose manual or the luxury of automatic operation a Gliderol roller door is very easy to operate. The doors incorporate a spring assisted mechanism in the roller providing precise balance and giving the user effortless operation. A fine balance between the weight of the curtain and the upward pull of the springs is maintained regardless of the position of the door. A manual door can be easily operated with just one hand.

Door goes up with one hand, door comes down with one hand.


Secure Locking 
The double sided locking system is operated from the central handle and locks from the inside and outside of the door.

Automatic Doors

Whilst many doors can be converted to automatic later there is significant cost saving if the unit is factory-fitted from the outset. Electric doors come with a push-button light assisted control unit, audible beep on open close and a keyring sized remote control for convenience.

Remote Contol

Glidematic remote control allows the door to be opened or closed by means of a compact hand transmitter, which works up to 100m away, or in the garage with the wall mounted push button. Either way, the door opens and closes as smoothly and quietly as you like. Extra transmitters are readily available (optional extra). You can use up to 10 additional transmitters on each garage door.

Push button, door goes up. Push button, door comes down.

Low Maintenance
The Gliderol door itself and the roller mechanism are designed to require extremely low maintenance. Wherever possible hard wearing nylon parts or bearings are used to reduce friction without the need for lubrication. Your door never needs to be lubricated since it glides effortlessly along its guide rails.
Convenience
The complete luxury of an automatic Gliderol door will spoil you forever. You don't event need to get out of the security and comfort of your own car to open / close the door.
Security and Fail-Safe
The unique rolling code circuitry ensures fail-safe security and eliminates the risk of another handset using the same code. A Glide-A-Code transmitter using UHF frequency, transmits a different code after every operation. Each time the handset and the control box move onto a new setting from 1 of 2,000,000,000 codes. This ensures the door cannot be operated by another transmitter. A connection to the household alarm is also available (optional extra).
Safety around children
The Glidermatic drive unit automatically reverses should the download movement of the door be restricted in any way. This ensures no accidents with little children and pets around.
Automatic light
The wall mounted remote unit houses a florescent light that illuminates your garage for up to 5 minutes after the door is activated. This allows you to drive into an illuminated garage and leave safely.
Quiet
As the Glidermatic drive unit is housed entirely within the door-roll noise emissions are substantially reduced resulting in a quiet and smoother performance, which also provides a greater space saving in the garage.

Sectional Doors

American style up and over sectional doors are also available. These quality doors open vertically so you can park your car directly in front of them and still open the door. They give excellent security with all round sealing and insulation. Available in paneled or horizontal design a sectional door, as the name implies, is made-up from separate sections rather than a single panel. This allows for exceptionally smooth operation both manually and especially when electrically operated.


Insulated Doors

An insulated version is also available. The insulated door curtain is constructed from 77mm foam filled aluminum slats approximately 15mm thick. All insulated doors come fully automated as standard. Insulated doors are ideal for garage conversion offering massive heat savings and noise dampening.
